# Latest Action Sprite Prompts

Shared style for all frames:

- High-resolution chibi pixel-art kabuki actor based on `web/assets/source/kabuki-actor-reference.png`.
- White kabuki mask with red markings, black hair mass, dark robe with red/purple trim, scarf/sleeves.
- Small Japanese message-kiosk/scroll inbox with cyan lantern and glowing message ribbons.
- Flat `#00ff00` chroma-key source background, then locally removed to transparent PNG.
- No text, no logos, no shadows, no floor plane, no watermark.

Frame sequence:

1. Actor stands alert beside the dim message kiosk, watching for a new update.
2. Kiosk notification light pulses while the actor leans in.
3. Actor opens the kiosk panel as a fresh glowing scroll emerges.
4. Actor pulls the fresh scroll from the slot.
5. Actor sweeps stale slips away while keeping the fresh scroll.
6. Actor turns a circular clock/timeline dial to verify freshness.
7. Kiosk bursts with cyan light and a message ribbon floats out.
8. Actor stamps the fresh ribbon as current.
9. Actor presents the sealed latest scroll proudly.
10. Actor returns to a loop-closing idle pose beside the now-lit kiosk.
