# Live Fire Mode Sprite Keyframes

Generated as individual image keyframes on a flat chroma green background, cleaned to transparent PNG frames, then assembled into `web/assets/mode-kabuki-live-fire-sheet.png`.

Baseline: the upgraded dark kabuki actor reference with the white/red mask, black hair mass, dark robe, red/purple trim, scarf movement, and readable high-resolution pixel-art proportions.

Motion arc:

1. Ready stance with tiny embers beginning around sleeves and feet.
2. Arms opening as embers spiral upward.
3. Lift-off with small flame jets under the feet.
4. Hover with a forming flame ring and faint glowing eyes.
5. Full flame-on bloom around the whole body.
6. Peak diagonal fire sweep across the sprite.
7. Controlled hover as the flame ring fades.
8. Descent with shrinking fire ribbons.
9. Low landing crouch with a small impact puff.
10. Rising out of the landing with embers receding.
11. Almost idle, faint eye glow, embers drifting away.
12. Final controlled fire stance with a tiny hand flame.
