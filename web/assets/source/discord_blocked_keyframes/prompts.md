# Discord Blocked Sprite Keyframes

Generated as individual image keyframes on a flat chroma green background, then cleaned to transparent PNG frames and assembled into `web/assets/scanner-kabuki-discord-blocked-sheet.png`.

Style constraints:

- High-detail pixel-art kabuki ninja actor.
- White kabuki mask with red markings, large black hair, black robe with crimson and deep purple trim.
- Small Japanese wooden gate/door with a visible lock.
- Clear blocked/sign-in-needed state without Discord branding or readable text.
- No readable text, no logos, no UI.

Motion plan:

1. Actor approaches the locked gate with a key/login scroll.
2. Actor tries the lock.
3. Lock rejects the key and the actor recoils.
4. Red checkpoint/no-entry seal appears.
5. Actor reads the reset/sign-in scroll.
6. Actor politely knocks at the locked gate.
7. Second denial flash knocks the actor back.
8. Actor sits dejected outside the locked gate.
9. Actor waits with lantern and scroll.
10. Actor returns to a hopeful retry pose.
