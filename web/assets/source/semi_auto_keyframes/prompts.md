# Semi Auto Mode Sprite Keyframes

Generated as individual image keyframes on a flat chroma green background, then cleaned to transparent PNG frames and assembled into `web/assets/mode-kabuki-semi-auto-sheet.png`.

Style constraints:

- High-detail pixel-art kabuki ninja actor.
- White kabuki mask with red markings, large black hair, black robe with crimson and deep purple trim.
- Small ornate theatrical prop pistol only; no firing, muzzle flash, targets, bullets, or impact effects.
- The prop stays controlled, angled upward/downward safely, or across the body.
- No readable text, no logos, no UI.

Motion plan:

1. Guarded ready stance, one hand near the robe fold.
2. Robe opens slightly.
3. Prop begins emerging across the torso.
4. Prop is drawn upward in a controlled pose.
5. Inspection/check pose.
6. Cock/check beat with a small glint.
7. Dramatic wait/approval signal with the prop held safely.
8. Prop lowers and begins returning to robe.
9. Prop slides back into robe.
10. Robe closes with a restrained approval gesture.
11. Final robe-close/sleeve-settle beat.
12. Stable guarded stance for a clean finish.
