# Posting Sprite Keyframes

Generated as individual image keyframes on a flat chroma green background, then cleaned to transparent PNG frames and assembled into `web/assets/scanner-kabuki-posting-sheet.png`.

Style constraints:

- High-detail pixel-art kabuki ninja actor.
- White kabuki mask with red markings, large black hair, black robe with crimson and deep purple trim.
- Clear full-body silhouette at topbar scale.
- No readable text, no logos, no UI.

Motion plan:

1. Ready stance with parchment envelope.
2. Crouched writing pose at a small table.
3. Folding the parchment into an envelope.
4. Stamping the envelope with a red seal.
5. Courier dash with the sealed envelope.
6. Reaching a Japanese-style wooden message box.
7. Pushing the envelope into the message box slot.
8. Message box burst with stars and a paper-bird send cue.
9. Thumbs-up/bow confirmation.
10. Return to ready stance for looping.
