# Repair Action Sprite Prompts

Shared style for all frames:

- High-resolution chibi pixel-art kabuki actor based on `web/assets/source/kabuki-actor-reference.png`.
- White kabuki mask with red markings, black hair mass, dark robe with red/purple trim, scarf/sleeves.
- Small Japanese shrine-like Discord channel signboard/control box with cyan circuitry and lantern.
- Flat `#00ff00` chroma-key source background, then locally removed to transparent PNG.
- No text, no logos, no shadows, no floor plane, no watermark.

Frame sequence:

1. Actor stands alert beside the broken shrine control box, inspecting it.
2. Actor crouches and pulls an oversized wrench from the robe/tool pouch.
3. Actor opens the side panel and inspects tangled cyan wires.
4. Actor leans in and wrenches inside the open panel.
5. Repair becomes comically intense with cyan/gold sparks.
6. Actor reconnects two glowing cyan cables with a bright snap.
7. Actor swings the repaired side panel closed.
8. Actor stamps a glowing repair seal onto the fixed box.
9. Actor steps back proudly as the fixed box glows fully.
10. Actor returns to a loop-closing idle pose beside the repaired box.
